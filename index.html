About Me

Hi! I'm Will Eccles, an 18-year-old software engineering student at Quinnipiac University from Tolland, CT. I have been programming since I was about 12 years old, when I started with BASIC. I have enjoyed programming and computer science ever since then, and over time, I have gained considerable knowledge of various programming languages and concepts. I like to write code that proves a concept, implements a theory, or just does something to prove that I can, but I also like to make software that makes life easier, whether it be for me or for others.

As far as operating systems are concerned, I use all of the major ones, including Windows, macOS, and Linux (preferrably Arch). I personally dislike Windows for productivity, and I mainly use it for gaming, but the others I use for any sort of productivity.

Even though I write a lot of code, that's not to say I don't have other hobbies! I enjoy swimming, customizing my computers (both software and hardware), shooting, and above all, mechanical keyboards. I am also open to trying new things—well, except for skydiving. That's just crazy.
